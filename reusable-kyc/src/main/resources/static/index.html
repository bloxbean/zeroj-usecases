Reusable KYC — BBS Selective Disclosure

Do KYC once. Reveal only what each service needs. Claim on-chain — the Cardano ledger verifies the BBS proof natively.

1 Issuer — sign the credential

A KYC provider verifies the subject once (out of band) and signs all five attributes with BBS. The holder stores it.

2 Holder — reveal only a subset

Pick what the verifier gets to see. The BBS proof still shows the issuer signed all attributes — the rest stay hidden.

3 Claim on-chain (Yaci DevKit)

A voucher is locked at the claim validator; spending it requires a BBS presentation the ledger itself verifies, disclosing country, kycLevel, paying the recipient.
